# **HomeCook Connection Hub** 🍽️ (HCCH)

**HomeCook Connection Hub** is a dynamic meal-sharing platform built on **Next.js** that connects home cooks with food enthusiasts. Whether you're a passionate chef looking to host meals or someone eager to enjoy a home-cooked dining experience, **HomeCook Connection Hub** makes it simple and enjoyable to share and discover great food.

--- ## 🚀 **Features**
- **Discover Local Meals**: Browse meals offered by local home chefs.
- **Host Your Own Meals**: Offer your culinary creations, manage bookings, and set availability easily.
- **Easy Reservations**: Seamlessly book meals with your favorite hosts.
- **User Reviews & Profiles**: Build trust with detailed user profiles, ratings, and reviews from both guests and hosts.


---

## 🛠 **Technologies Used**
- **Next.js**: Frontend
- **Node.js**: Backend
- **PostgreSQL** Database
- **Render.com** Deployment
